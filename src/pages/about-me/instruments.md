I play guitars for fun and I used to play drums in a load of bands. My drum kit was the only pro-level gear I've owned and I'm not sure it ever earned its own value, thanks to the shiny new things coming out of Poole Percussion and Fret Music that always tempted me to "increase my investment". I wouldn't change a thing, though.

I played guitar and bass in a few bands, but just for the fun of it.

## Current gear

## Practice Pads: Carlsbro Compact Electronic Kit

When I sold my drum kit (a Premier/Zildjian powerhouse) I missed playing so much I had to get a little practice kit. I don't have much space, so this entry-level kit was perfect. I stick tracks through the aux input with a bluetooth received and play along. I've had it a few years and it might be getting towards end-of-life, mostly due to me playing it hard.

- Dual zone snare
- 3x 8" toms
- Hi-hat
- 2x cymbals
- 2x pedal controllers
