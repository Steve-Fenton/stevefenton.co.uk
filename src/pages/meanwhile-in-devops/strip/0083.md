Ellis and Blake are reviewing their code changes and burning their new software version to CD.

"We've made this pretty hard to test," Blake says.

"Never mind," Ellis replies. "Check it over the fence to DevOps."

Ellis flips the CD like a Frisbee, where it arcs through the air and smacks Blake on the head.

"Ouch!" Blake exclaims. "You know that we *are* DevOps, right?"

:::

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
