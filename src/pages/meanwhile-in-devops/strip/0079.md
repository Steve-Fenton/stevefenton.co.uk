A fire has broken out in the server room. Smoke billows from the open door, and one of the team members spots it.

"Fire!"

The team member rushes to the fire alarm point, which has a glass panel that reads "In case of emergency break glass," with arrows pointing to the center, where it's easiest to break.

They punch the glass.

The fire alarm screen updates to say "We value your privacy. We'd like to share your data with 3,694 selected partners," with a green "Okay" button and a subtle "More options" button.

The office continues to burn.

:::

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
