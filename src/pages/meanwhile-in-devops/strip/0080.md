Will is on stage at a tech conference. Behind him is a photograph from the 1960s.

"In the sixties, girls would wear rollers everywhere," he begins. "To the shops, to church, even on dates."

As he talks, he clicks through various slides showing women with curlers in their hair everywhere they go.

"There's no evidence they ever took them out..."

As Will is talking, an audience member raises their hand to interrupt.

"Excuse me," they call from the seating area. "I thought this talk was about SBOMs?"

:::

<small>This story was inspired by the story of hair curlers told by William Zinsser (On Writing Well) and by all the organizations generating SBOMs but doing nothing with them.</small>

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
