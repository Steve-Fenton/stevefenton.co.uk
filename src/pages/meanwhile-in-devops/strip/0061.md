Adley is cleaning the house and needs to vacuum the carpet.

"The vacuum cleaner won't switch on?!" Adley says while flicking the switch. Click! Click!

Blake sits at a computer. A notification pops up.

"Time for my Zoom call," Blake says and launches the call.

Suddenly, there's an "Aha" and Adley's vacuum cleaner whirs into life, just as Blake connects.

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
