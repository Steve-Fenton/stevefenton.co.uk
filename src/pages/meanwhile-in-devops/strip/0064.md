Doug is in the golden goose room with his clipboard. Doug's clipboard is never a good sign. The goose is stood on the table looking at Doug. A bowl of food is on the table next to the goose.

"If I reduce food costs per half," Doug muses. "We'll increase the golden egg margin by 3%."

Doug switches out the larger bowl of food with one much smaller. This will save the company a lot of money.

Doug returns the next day to find the goose on its back, its legs stiffly pointing toward the ceiling.

:::

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
