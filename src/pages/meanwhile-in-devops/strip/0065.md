Blake is taking a stroll and enjoying the fresh air when there's a sudden pounding of feet. A group of people, including Murray, Doug, Adley, and Ellis, is running full tilt. They look panicked.

"What's going on?" Blake calls out.

"Hurry up," Doug shouts as he flies past. "Come on! You're going to be left behind!"

Blake watches as they rush over the edge of a cliff and fall into the sea, the same expressions of stress and excitement still plastered across their faces.
