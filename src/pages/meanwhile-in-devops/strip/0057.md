Blake is on a Zoom call with Arlo. They are about to disconnect.

"See you next week at the conference," Blake says.

"Yeah," Arlo replies. "Catch you next week."

The following week arrives, and they meet at the conference.

"Wow," exclaims Blake. "You're a lot taller than you look on Zoom!"

:::
