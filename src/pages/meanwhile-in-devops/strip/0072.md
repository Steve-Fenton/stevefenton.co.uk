Clarke is talking to Ellis and Blake when he spots movement from the corner of his eye.

"Did you see that?" Clarke asks.

"Nope," Ellis says lethargically.

"No," adds Blake.

They continue chatting until another distraction catches Clarke's attention.

"Did you hear that?" Clarke asks.

"Nothing," sighs Ellis.

"Still no," Blake adds.

And that's what it's really like to have superpowers.

:::

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
