Ellis is presenting his genius strategy for boosting revenue at his tech company. He's sharing a slide with Van Gogh's sunflowers.

"Some paintings sell for millions," Ellis explains.

He switches to a slide showing the amateur restoration of the Ecce Homo from the Sanctuary of Mercy.

"So," Ellis continues. "If we can boost artist productivity, we'll be rich. 10x more paintings. 10x more revenue, right?"

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
