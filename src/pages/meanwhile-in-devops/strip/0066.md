Arlo is showing people around the Museum of Detective Fiction Legends. He pauses by a portrait of a regal woman with angular glasses and curly gray hair.

"Agatha Christie wrote 66 detective novels," Arlo explains. "And she sold over two billion copies!"

"I don't get why she's so popular," Doug laments. "I've written 80 detective novels!"
