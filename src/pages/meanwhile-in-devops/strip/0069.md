A programmer is working through a tough problem. They have a large screen filled with code, and they are tapping away on their mechanical keyboard. A small yellow rubber duck sits below the screen, and the programmer addresses it aloud as they work.

"So, Duckie, the bug comes from this function," the developer says. "What might cause..."

The developer pauses for a second and then says in a celebratory tone: "Aha! Found it!"

Zoom in on the rubber duck, who has a thought bubble: "Took you long enough, fool!"

:::

## Meanwhile in DevOps

The *Meanwhile in DevOps* series is an ill-conceived and badly drawn comic strip about software delivery.

I'm many things, but not an artist. It's doubtful whether I'm funny, too.
