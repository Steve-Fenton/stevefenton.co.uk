Join us for Introducing the AI Pulse Report, a concise, insight-driven webinar that breaks down the findings and implications of Octopus Deploy's AI Pulse Report: AI adoption and its impact on developer productivity. The 2026 report combines global survey data from technology professionals with industry research to reveal how AI is reshaping software delivery workflows, hiring trends, and long-term talent dynamics.

In this session, you'll learn:

- What's really happening with AI in software development: real adoption patterns, where AI is delivering measurable productivity gains, and where it isn't.
- Hiring and workforce impacts: how AI use is shifting demand across experience levels, the emerging "seniors + AI" model, and potential risks to the developer talent pipeline.
- Structural insights: why productivity gains at the task level don't always translate to end-to-end delivery improvements and how automation practices like Continuous Delivery act as a multiplier for AI value.

A forward-looking view: strategic recommendations for teams that want to integrate AI responsibly — balancing tooling, talent development, and delivery practices.

Whether you're building with AI today or planning your next investment, this webinar distills the AI Pulse Report's key research into actionable insights and context for technology leaders and delivery teams.
